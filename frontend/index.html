Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1: How can I contribute to the cause?

A: You can contribute by donating money or clothes. For monetary contributions, you can choose from three tiers: Basic ($5), Plus ($25), Pro ($120), or enter a custom amount. You can also donate clothes by selecting the type and quantity.

Q2: Can I choose my own donation amount?

A: Yes! In addition to the preset amounts (Basic, Plus, and Pro), you can enter any amount you want starting from $1 through the custom donation option.

Q3: How do I donate clothes?

A: To donate clothes, simply select the type of clothes (children, women, or men) and the number of items you'd like to donate. You can donate any number of items starting from 1.

Q4: How are my contributions used?

A: All monetary contributions are used to provide food to people in need based on the selected tier. Clothes donations are distributed to those who need them the most, according to their region and demographic needs.

Q5: Can I track my contributions?

A: Yes, your contributions are tracked, and you will earn points based on your contributions. You can view your rank on the global leaderboard, which showcases top volunteers.

Q6: How are points awarded?

A: Points are awarded based on your contributions. The more you contribute, the higher your points. These points help you rank globally among other volunteers.

Q7: Can I see live statistics of the contributions?

A: Yes! We provide live charts that show the real-time contributions made by users. You can also see statistics on poverty, including data based on gender, age, and region.

Q8: Is there a way to sign up and stay informed?

A: Yes, you can sign up on our platform to create an account. This allows you to log in, track your contributions, and stay updated with the latest news and developments.

Q9: Can I cancel or modify a donation?

A: Unfortunately, once a donation is made, it cannot be canceled. However, you can always make additional contributions if you'd like to increase your support!
